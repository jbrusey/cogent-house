Component: tos.platforms.telosa.chips.sht11.SensirionSht11C

generic configuration SensirionSht11C()

SensirionSht11C is a top-level access component for the Sensirion SHT11 model humidity and temperature sensor, available on the telosb platform. Because this component represents one physical device, simultaneous calls to read temperature and humidity will be arbitrated and executed in sequential order. Feel free to read both at the same time, just be aware that they'll come back sequentially.
