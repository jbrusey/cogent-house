Component: tos.platforms.telosa.chips.sht11.HplSensirionSht11C

configuration HplSensirionSht11C

HplSensirionSht11C is a low-level component, intended to provide the physical resources used by the Sensirion SHT11 sensor on the telosb platform so that the chip driver can make use of them. You really shouldn't be wiring to this, unless you're writing a new Sensirion SHT11 driver.
